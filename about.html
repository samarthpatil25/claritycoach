About

Why this works differently, and what to expect from me.

I spend my time paying attention to how people talk themselves in circles, through the content they consume, the conversations they replay, the stories they repeat until they believe them.

Most of us are surprisingly good at solving the wrong problem. We fix the symptom, feel better for a week, and then find ourselves right back where we started, because the thing underneath was never actually named.

These conversations are where that changes. One-on-one, online, and completely confidential, applied to your actual life instead of a script. I'll listen closely, ask the questions that matter, and be honest with you about what I'm hearing, even when it's the uncomfortable version.

And if something you're facing would be better served by a professional, I'll say so, and help point you toward the right kind of support.
